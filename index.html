📖 How to Use the Downloader
1

Copy Post Link

Navigate to YouTube, Instagram, or Facebook and copy the link of any public post, video, reel, or short.

2

Paste URL or Focus Input

Click Paste to insert the URL, or simply click/focus the input box to automatically paste matching URLs from your clipboard.

3

Select Resolution / Tab

Switch between MP4 Video, MP3 Audio, or WebM tabs to see available formats, bitrates, and dynamic file sizes.

4

Download Media

Confirm compliance and click Download. The progress bar will count to 100% directly on the row button, saving the file to your device.
